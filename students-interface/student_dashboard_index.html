📊 Skill Ability Profile

🎯 Major Skills — 9 specialized competencies of your selected category. Example: Accounting tracks Financial Reporting, Auditing, Taxation, etc. Shows your depth in your major.

🌐 General Skills (9 universal) — PK = average of your major skills (overall depth). QA Quantitative · DI Data · CM Communication · IP Innovation · ER Ethics · TL Teamwork · IC Intercultural · SL Self-directed.

How scores are computed — each course's outcome/syllabus is matched against keyword lists for every skill dimension. Score = coverage (65%) + depth (20%) + specificity (15%), rounded to 0–100. Red mark on each row = your selected category's average (only counting courses where the skill > 0).
